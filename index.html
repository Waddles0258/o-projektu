Welcome to Seven Continents Safari!

TASK

  • Educational site for children on the topic of the geography of our planet.
  • The site should give children an idea of ​​the continents and the animals that live on these continents.
  • Provide an opportunity to train your memory in a game format.

SOLUTION

  • A video that tells about the continents in an accessible form.
  • Clickable map with continents and clickable animal figures.
  • A game for training your memory and remembering information about continents and animals.
